## Metathesis

A word or phrase becomes another when two letters are interchanged. Examples: <Example>converse, conserve</Example> and <Example>fine arts, fire ants</Example>.

In a **[reversed](modifier/reversed) metathesis**, a word or phrase becomes another when two letters are interchanged and the result is reversed. Example: <Example>oompahs, shampoo</Example>.
