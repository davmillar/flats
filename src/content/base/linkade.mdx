## Linkade

A word or phrase is broken into two or more shorter parts, which overlap by one letter. Examples: <Example><Step>philately</Step> <Finally>Phi*l*, *l*ately</Finally></Example> and <Example><Step>libraries</Step> <Finally>Libr*a*, *A*ries</Finally></Example>.
