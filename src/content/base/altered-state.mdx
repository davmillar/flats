## Altered state

A two-letter state abbreviation is replaced by another. Examples: <Example><Step>s<del>pa</del></Step> <Finally>s<ins>ky</ins></Finally></Example>; <Example><Step>prov<del>id</del>e</Step> <Finally>prov<ins>ok</ins>e</Finally></Example>. (Answer keys in the form of &ldquo;s-pa/ky&rdquo; or &ldquo;prov-id/ok-e&rdquo;.)
